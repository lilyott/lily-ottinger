I write about the political economy of authoritarian states. As Managing Editor and Taiwan Analyst for ChinaTalk, I cover China, Taiwan, technology, and geopolitics.

My work spans original reporting, analysis, and on-the-ground dispatches from across Asia and Central Asia — from Taipei to Kyrgyzstan, Seoul to Chongqing.
