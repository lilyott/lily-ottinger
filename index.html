I write about the political economy of authoritarian states. As Managing Editor and Taiwan Analyst for ChinaTalk, I cover China, Taiwan, technology, and geopolitics.

My work spans original reporting, analysis, and on-the-ground dispatches from across Asia — from Taipei to Kyrgyzstan, Seoul to Chongqing.

Cross-Strait Crisis: A Strategic Simulation
An interactive geopolitical strategy game simulating decision-making during a potential military crisis between China, Taiwan, and the United States. Players make policy decisions across 20 quarters (2026–2030), with cascading consequences for military readiness, diplomacy, and crisis escalation. Funded by an Emergent Ventures grant.
